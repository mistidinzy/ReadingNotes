Hello.

My name is Misti Dinzy.

I’m 29 years old. I have a background in Fraud Prevention and Quality Assurance.

I’m still unsure of my goals. I started working when I was 14, and it feels like that’s all there has been. Work.

I grew up glued to PBS, so I can't say I haven't been encouraged to "follow my dreams" and "be myself".

But real life doesn’t always follow the motivational poster plan.


This is the first time in my life where I’ve been able to make career decisions that are motivated by my passions.

My interests, my dreams. The things that keep me up at night.

So the waters are new, and I wouldn’t say that I know myself half as well as I should.

Somethings I know are that I over-think. I want to understand everything.

I want to help and I want to fix things.

We have such a seemingly unending bounty of unique tech, and it’s always growing.

Sometimes it feels like there couldn’t possibly be anything more to contribute.

And yet, at some point each day, I always think,

“This would be (better, easier, faster, more accessible, etc.) if…”

"I wrote my way out."
